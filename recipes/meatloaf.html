Meatloaf

This ia a dish called meatloaf

Meatloaf is a dish of ground meat that has been combined with other ingredients and formed into the shape of a loaf, then baked or smoked

INGRIDENTS

  • 2 lbs ground beef, 85% or 90% lean
  • 1 med onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 Tbsp ketchup
  • 3 Tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 3/4 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 ½ tsp salt, or to taste
  • 1 ½ tsp Italian seasoning
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground paprika

Steps in making a meatloaf recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, bread crumbs and egg in a large bowl;
  3. season with salt and pepper. Transfer into prepared loaf pan.
  4. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a small bowl until well combined;
  5. pour over meatloaf and spread it evenly over the top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
