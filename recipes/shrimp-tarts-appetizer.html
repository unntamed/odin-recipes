shrimp-tarts-recipe

The recipe shrimp tarts is a delicious dish, prepared in about 5-30 minutes.

Although this recipe has many different ways for preparation...

INGRIDENTS

  • 1/2 cup Original Bisquick™ mix
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked shrimp
  • 4 medium green onions, sliced (1/4 cup)

steps in making shrimp-tarts-appetizers

  1. Heat oven to 400°F.
  2. Spray 24 miniature (1 3/4x1 inch) muffin cups with cooking spray.
  3. In medium bowl, beat Bisquick mix, milk, sour cream, Worcestershire sauce and egg with spoon until blended.
  4. Stir in remaining ingredients.
  5. Spoon about 1 tablespoon mixture into each muffin cup.
  6. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until golden.
  7. cool 5 minutes
  8. Loosen sides of tarts from pan; remove from pan.
